### What we do

**Nusio** is a digital eSIM storefront built for travellers heading to Indonesia.
Pick a data plan, complete a quick identity check (required for Indonesian SIM
registration), pay by card, and receive your eSIM QR code by email — usually
within a couple of hours, any time of day.

### How it works

1. **Choose a plan** at [nusio.id](https://nusio.id)
2. **Verify your identity** — a passport/ID photo, as required for SIM registration in Indonesia
3. **Pay securely** by card
4. **Scan your QR** — we email the eSIM once it's activated, and you're online the moment you land
